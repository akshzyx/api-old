Guide


1. Request your data from Spotify

Navigate to spotify.com/account and scroll down to "Download your data", and click the "Request" button.

2. Wait till your data arrives in your email

As Spotify states:

We are preparing your data file. This can take up to 30 days to complete.

You'll receive an email when it is ready to download.

3. Extract the .ZIP file

Spotify delivers your data in a .ZIP file, which includes a lot of personal information which you dont want to share with others. Spotistats only needs the files named like StreamingHistory_X.json
